Virtual Lab Website

Introduction

Welcome to our virtual lab website! Our website offers three experiments that you can explore and learn from. These experiments are:

  • AC control unit
  • Faraday's experiment simulator
  • Hysteresis curve plotter

In this manual, we will guide you through each of these experiments and show you how to use them.

AC Control Unit

Getting Started

  1. Go to the AC control unit experiment page.
  2. Enter your location and the current day of the week.
  3. Press the "Submit" button to get the current temperature and humidity levels for your location.

AC Operation

  1. Depending upon the location's temperature and the current day and time, ac will be turned on or off
  2. Press the "Submit" button to apply the changes.
  3. The state of the AC and temperature and humidity levels will be displayed on the screen.

Faraday's Experiment Simulator

Getting Started

  1. Go to the Faraday's experiment simulator page.
  2. Click and drag the magnet to move it in and out of the coil.
  3. Observe the induced emf and current values displayed on the screen.

Hysteresis Curve Plotter

Getting Started

  1. Go to the Hysteresis curve plotter page.
  2. Select the material you want to plot from the dropdown menu.
  3. Press the "Plot" button to plot the hysteresis curve.

Changing the Material

  1. Select a different material from the menu.
  2. Corresponding Plot will be generated according to experimental data.

Inference

  1. Ferromgnetic properties of the material can be analyzed.
